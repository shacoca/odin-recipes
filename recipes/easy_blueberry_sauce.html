Easy Blueberry Sauce

Made this for my kids to serve over pancakes. Also great as a sauce for grilling. Quick, easy and delicious.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup fresh blueberries
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch (Optional)

Steps

  1. Combine blueberries, sugar, water, and cornstarch in a saucepan; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until thickened, about 10 minutes.
